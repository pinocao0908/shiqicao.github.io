About

I received my B.S. in Mathematics and Applied Mathematics from Zhengzhou University (2024). I am currently a master’s student at the Center for Combinatorics, Nankai University.

My current research interests focus on studying and addressing the log-concavity of matroid-related polynomials and sequences by leveraging tools such as Lorentzian polynomial theory and AI-based algorithms.
